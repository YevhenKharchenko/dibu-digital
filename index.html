Industry Roast

The digital world is full of trends, but let’s be real – not all of them make sense. We’re here to expose the good, the bad, and the ridiculous in digital marketing.

  • "Let’s Go Viral" Syndrome: Every brand wants a viral video, but let’s be honest – virality isn’t a strategy; it’s luck.

  • Endless A/B Testing: Do we really need 200 ad variations, or are we just afraid to commit?

  • The "Make the Logo Bigger" Obsession: Because nothing says brand trust like a logo taking up 90% of the screen.

  • SEO Overload: Keyword stuffing is out; high-quality, user-focused content is in. Stop writing for robots.

  • The Social Media Grind: Posting daily on every platform? If the content isn’t engaging, does it even matter?

At Dibu Digital, we don’t follow trends blindly. We craft strategies that actually make sense for your brand.
